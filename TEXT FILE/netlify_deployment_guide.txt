SmartAttend Deployment & PWA Installation Guide (Netlify)
==========================================================
Date Created: May 22, 2026

To turn SmartAttend into a mobile app that students and professors can download (PWA - Progressive Web App), the project must be hosted online. 

Why Netlify?
------------
Mobile browsers (Android Chrome / iOS Safari) have strict security rules:
1. The "Install App" feature (triggered by `manifest.json` and `sw.js`) will ONLY work on a secure HTTPS connection.
2. The Face Recognition webcam will ONLY turn on over a secure HTTPS connection.
Local WiFi networks use insecure HTTP, which is why the app cannot be installed directly from your computer to your phone. Netlify provides the free, mandatory HTTPS connection.

How to Deploy to Netlify (Drag and Drop Method):
------------------------------------------------
1. Go to the website: https://drop.netlify.com/
2. Drag and drop your entire `SmartAttend2@` folder into the upload box on the page.
3. Within 10 seconds, Netlify will generate a live, secure HTTPS link (e.g., https://yoursite.netlify.app).
4. Share this link with your students and professors.

How Users Install the App:
--------------------------
1. A user opens the Netlify link on their phone's browser.
2. Because Netlify provides a secure HTTPS connection, the browser allows `sw.js` to run.
3. The browser reads `manifest.json` and automatically triggers a pop-up: "Add SmartAttend to Home Screen".
4. The user clicks "Add", and SmartAttend installs natively onto their phone, functioning like a regular app with camera permissions enabled!
